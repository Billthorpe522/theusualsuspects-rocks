What to have ready when you book

To speed up the call, have these in front of you:

  • The date (and a backup date if your first choice doesn't work)
  • The venue name and address (so we can plan load-in + travel time)
  • The room — indoor/outdoor, stage size, expected crowd size
  • The hours — when doors open, when we play, when you want us done
  • Your budget — we'll tell you honestly if it's a fit

What it costs

Honest answer: it depends. The base rate depends on:

  • Distance from our home base (Manhattan, IL) — travel over 50 miles adds per-mile
  • Hours — standard is 3 hours of music; longer is more
  • Day of the week — Friday/Saturday are premium; weekday gigs are often discounted
  • Whether you want our PA — adds $200 if so
  • Holidays — Thanksgiving weekend, NYE, July 4th weekend are premium rates

Buck will quote you a flat number on the phone. No surprise add-ons. If something changes (you extend the hours, the venue moves), Buck will tell you before the change happens.
