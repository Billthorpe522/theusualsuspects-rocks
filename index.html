Common questions from venue bookers

How far does The Usual Suspects travel?

Standard travel within 50 miles of our home base (Manhattan, IL). That covers Frankfort, Joliet, New Lenox, Tinley Park, Manteno, Bradley, Mokena, Dyer, Schererville, and most of the south suburbs Chicago + NW Indiana. Further on request.

Do you bring your own PA system?

Yes — PA available on request for an additional $200. Suitable for rooms up to ~250 people. If your venue has its own PA, we're happy to use that.

How long is a typical set?

Three 45-minute sets with a 30-minute dinner break in the middle is standard. We can compress to two sets for shorter gigs, or extend for longer ones.

What genres do you play?

Classic rock, R&B/soul, country (modern and classic), and slow songs/ballads. We don't play heavy metal or pre-1990 country. See the full setlist.

Do you have insurance?

Yes — general liability ($1M occurrence / $2M aggregate). We can name your venue as additional insured for an additional $50. Send us your venue's COI requirements when you book.

What's your cancellation policy?

30+ days out: no fee. 14–30 days: 25%. Under 14 days: 50%. Day-of: full fee. We rarely cancel — but if we have to, you get a full refund.
