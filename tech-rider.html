Stage notes:

  • Bass amp stage right (audience left)
  • Lead vocal mic + guitar amp stage left (audience right)
  • Rhythm guitar amp center stage right
  • All vocal mics on boom stands
  • Monitor wedges: 4 (one per musician position)

If your room isn't standard

Most VFW halls, American Legion posts, and bars are standard. But if your venue has any of these, tell us in advance so we can plan:

  • Multi-floor venue with stage upstairs (load-in via stairs — let us know if there's an elevator)
  • Outdoor stage with no roof (we'll play in light rain, but thunderstorms cancel — need a rain plan)
  • Acoustic-sensitive room (we'll keep stage volume reasonable; bring foam wedges if you have them)
  • Limited power (only 1× 20A circuit available — we can run a lean setup, but ask first)
  • Sound ordinance in your town (we'll keep stage volume under their threshold)
  • Venue insurance requirements (we have general liability; send us your COI requirements when you book)
